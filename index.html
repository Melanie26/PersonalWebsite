Work

While at university, I worked on a number of individual and group projects:
My dissertation foced on user-centred design: An ethnographic study on the construction and use of shopping lists. Research was conducted in the UK, USA and South Africa. From the research findings- taking an iterative approach- I produced a number of lo-fi prototypes including storyboards, sketches, mock-ups etc. User feedback being central throughout the project. The end product was a mid-fi prototype produced in MXML and ActionScript, which combined all the work processes in constructing and using a shopping list into one app.
For my second year group project: We developed a web-based tool which aids module selection for exchange students at the University of Nottingham. My role in this project mostly comprised of the documentation, liaising with the development and testing teams throughout.
Other module projects included:

  • Wireframes and a mid-fi prototype of a restaurant review app for the HCI module.
  • Lo-fi, mid-fi and hi-fi prototypes of a collaborative research tool- "WeSearch"
  • Lo-fi and mid-fi module review tool for students at the University of Nottingham- "Module Share"
